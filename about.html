About This Website

This website is a personal poetry space created to share Assamese poems. Most of the poems published here are original works written by the author, while some are shared as Guest Poems with proper credit.

Poetry is a way to express emotions, thoughts, and life experiences. Through this website, the author aims to connect with readers who relate to simple feelings, deep thoughts, and everyday emotions.

This website is managed by a student who loves writing and learning. The journey has just started, and more poems will be added regularly.

Thank you for reading and supporting this poetry journey.

✍️ Author: Rituraj Saikia
